WTBU – Interview with The Needle Drop’s Anthony Fantano

05/11/2015

Antony Fantano, aka ‘the internet’s busiest music nerd’ of the theneedledrop.com, is an acclaimed music critic and radio personality. Fantano combines comedy and insightful commentary in his reviews of the latest music across a variety of genres. In this interview with WTBU’s Matt Garamella, Anthony offers his insights on how to get noticed on college radio, today’s music, and much more. Listen below!
